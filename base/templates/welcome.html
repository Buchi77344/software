Instructions for Computer-Based Testing (CBT)

  1. Read each question carefully before selecting your answer.
  2. All questions are mandatory; ensure you answer all of them before submitting.
  3. You can review your answers before the final submission.
  4. Regularly monitor the timer to avoid being logged out when time expires.
  5. Click the "Submit" button once you have completed all the questions.
  6. If you encounter any technical issues, please contact support immediately.
  7. Keyboard Shortcuts:
    • Press N for "Next" question
    • Press R for "Previous" question
    • Press A for option "A"
    • Press B for option "B"
    • Press C for option "C"
    • Press D for option "D"
    • Press S to submit the test

Note: Once you submit your test, you will not be able to make any changes.
